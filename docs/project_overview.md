# Project Overview

## Project Name

**Financial Complaint Analytics & Risk Monitoring Dashboard**

---

## Objective

Build an executive-ready Power BI dashboard that helps financial services stakeholders monitor complaint activity, response timeliness, company concentration, operational risk indicators, category drivers, and data quality controls.

The project is designed to demonstrate how Power BI can support financial complaint monitoring, risk prioritization, operational reporting, and governance-ready analytics.

## Business Context

Complaint monitoring is important in financial services because it connects customer experience, operational performance, compliance readiness, and risk management.

A finance, risk, or operations team needs to understand:

- Where complaint volume is concentrated
- Whether responses are handled on time
- Which companies or categories show elevated risk indicators
- Which recurring issues require review
- Whether reporting assumptions and data quality controls are transparent

This dashboard simulates how a financial services analytics team could monitor complaint operations and surface areas requiring management attention.

## Business Questions Answered

This dashboard answers the following business questions:

1. What is the total complaint volume and how does it trend over time?
2. Which complaint categories drive the highest volume?
3. Which companies have the highest complaint concentration?
4. Are complaint responses being handled within expected timeliness standards?
5. Which companies show higher late-response or operational risk indicators?
6. Which complaint issues appear most frequently?
7. Which categories or companies require deeper investigation?
8. Are data quality assumptions transparent enough for finance reporting?
9. How can AI visuals support directional risk-driver exploration?

## High-Risk Definition

A complaint is classified as high risk if it meets at least one of the following conditions:

- The company response was late
- The complaint narrative contains at least 300 words

This business-rule definition is used for KPI reporting, risk monitoring, company prioritization, and the AI Insights page.

The high-risk flag is not a regulatory classification. It is an analytical indicator used to prioritize review.
